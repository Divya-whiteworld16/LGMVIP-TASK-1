About

Gwalior is known for its fortress ruled by varius north-indian kingdoms. It is situated in the heart of india i.e. Madhya Pradesh state.

Gwalior is the major city in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh having the population of 2.6 million (approx). Gwalior is known for its beautiful and historic Fort, which is build in 10 century.Gwalior Fort was also called pearl necklace of the India by Mughal Emperor, Babur.... Beside this there are many other beautiful places in Gwalior. Jai vilas palace , Tansen Museum, Musical Fountain ,Sun Temple and many more. To praise the heavenliness of Gwalior please do visit.
